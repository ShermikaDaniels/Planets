Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and does not have a solid surface, its surface is made up of gas which is mainly Helium. Saturn also has rings that are also not solid and are made up of bits of ice, dust, and rock. Saturn has more moons than any other planet, it has 85 confirmed moons. Saturn has a moon that is bigger than Mercury that is named Titan. A day on Saturn is 10.7 hours and a year is 29 Earth years.
